Academic partners

Our academic partners in Ecuador are two public universities located in the two largest Ecuadorian Andes cities (Quito and Cuenca): the Escuela Politécnica Nacional (and the Universidad de Cuenca).
The project constitutes one of the first collaborative research initiatives between these two institutions.
